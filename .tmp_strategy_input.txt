9:btc perpetual futures, 15-minute timeframe with session-anchored vwap (anchored to utc 00:00). short entry: price closes >= vwap + 2 session-stdev and rsi(14) > 75 and prior bar is a bearish reversal (lower close than open); enter short on next bar open. long entry: mirror - price closes <= vwap - 2 session-stdev and rsi(14) < 25 and prior bar is bullish reversal. stop loss: vwap + 3 session-stdev for shorts, vwap - 3 session-stdev for longs. take profit: exit 70 percent of position at vwap touch, trail remainder with 0.5 x atr(14) stop. time stop: exit fully if no progress toward vwap within 5 bars (about 75 minutes). position sizing: risk 1 percent of account per trade based on stop distance. max one open position. filter: skip the day entirely if absolute price minus vwap greater than 5 percent (overextended regime). source: https://www.tradewink.com/learn/mean-reversion-strategy and https://www.hyrotrader.com/blog/vwap-trading-strategy/
